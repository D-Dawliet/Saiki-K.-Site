MC: Kusuo Saiki

“ Good grief. やれやれ-yare yare ”
—Kusuo's common catchphrase.

Kusuo Saiki (斉木 楠雄, さいき くすお lit. Saiki Kusuo) is the main protagonist of Saiki Kusuo no Ψ Nan who has all kinds of psychic related abilities. He usually tries to avoid getting involved in problematic or attention-grabbing situations, but his attempts always end up going wrong or having unusual ends to his displeasure. His trademark appearance contains his constant frown, his green-tinted glasses and two pink antennas located slightly above each ear, which have the function of limiting his power. Initially he was always seen alone, but in the course of the story he develops lasting friendships with his classmates and schoolmates, especially with Riki Nendou and Shun Kaido, or with his neighbors like Yuuta Iridatsu. Kusuo was starred by Kento Yamazaki in the live action movie based on the series.

Appearance: Kusuo has an average build and height. He has short pink hair, fair skin, and purple eyes. Kusuo wears a pair of controller devices, one on each side of his head, which are never removed except in situations of absolute necessity or by accident. The devices resemble white, cyan antennae, with pink, lollipop-like spheres. Accompanying the devices, Kusuo wears white glasses with lime green lenses to prevent his petrification power. During the days he has school, he wears the standard PK Academy uniform that all male students wear. On his days off from school, he occasionally wears clothes that are sewn by his mother. However, there are times he is forced to buy clothes since his mother has an exotic taste in clothing patterns.

Personality & traits: When Kusuo was a child, he was more open to his emotions. Part of it was due to the fact that he was unaware of what his powers did, such as breaking a whack-a-mole machine in an arcade and thinking the machine was giving him praise. He was highly mischievous, such as making his parents look for him for 5 hours after he teleported, and smiled around others without a second thought. Father: Kuniharu Saiki




Kuniharu Saiki(斉木 國春, さいき くにはる lit. Saiki Kuniharu) is the father of Kusuo and Kusuke and the husband of Kurumi. Kuniharu is one of the supporting characters of the series and was starred by Tanabe Seiichi in the live action of the series.
Appearance: Kuniharu often wears a suit and tie when going to work. He sports a pair of glasses and has brown hair and eyes, a change from his first appearance in the one-shot volume of the series where he has black hair and eyes.
Personality & traits:Kuniharu is unreliable, reckless and slovenly, often begging with disgusting teary faces towards his son. He is a doormat who is always nice and respectful to people no matter how rude or scornful they are to him, such as his boss, who orders him to lick his shoes, and his father-in-law, who harbors a strong hatred towards him. Mother: Kurumi Saiki




Kurumi Saiki (斉木 久留美, さいき くるみ lit. Saiki Kurumi) is the mother of Kusuo and Kusuke and the wife of Saiki Kuniharu.Kurumi is one of the supporting characters of the series and was starred by Uchida Yuki in the live action of the series.
Appearance: Kurumi is considerably younger looking for her age (37 years old) like her mother Saiki Kumi and has a kindly appearance. She sports dark purple eyes and short black hair that is styled exactly like Kusuo's, the only difference being that her hair is slightly longer. In the one-shot volume of the series, she sported long black hair which reached her waist and was tied into a ponytail with a ribbon.

Big Brother: Kusuke Saiki




Kusuke Saiki (斉木 空助, さいき くうすけ lit. Saiki Kūsuke) is Kusuo's older brother, a super genius who likes to invent machines/devices. He is 2 years older than Kusuo.
Appearance: Kusuke, like Kusuo, was born with a different hair color than his parents. He has blonde hair and slightly darker eyes. He wears his "Telepathy Canceler" whenever he is around Kusuo. When working, he is mostly seen with his machines, wearing a typical white lab coat worn by scientists. When in school, he wears a long black robe with a white collar buttoned up, and while on his days off, he wears normal clothes such as a t-shirt and pants.
